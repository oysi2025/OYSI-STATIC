REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals) is the EU regulation for the registration and evaluation of chemicals.

GHS (Globally Harmonized System) is the globally harmonized system for the classification and labeling of chemicals – recognizable by the hazard pictograms.

ADR (Accord européen relatif au transport international des marchandises Dangereuses par Route) regulates the safe transport of dangerous goods by road.

All our products are fully compliant with these regulations.

The OYSI Chemistry Lexicon is a public knowledge database with over 3,800 chemical substances. You will find:

  • CAS numbers and chemical formulas
  • GHS classification and pictograms
  • Safety information
  • Physical and chemical properties

The lexicon is freely accessible and requires no registration.
