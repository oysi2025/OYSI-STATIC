Features

What our DPP delivers

QR Code Identification

Every product, every batch receives a unique QR code. Scan with smartphone – instant access to all data.

Compliance Documents

Safety data sheets, GHS classifications, REACH status, analysis certificates – all linked and up-to-date.

Batch Traceability

From raw material to end customer. LOT numbers, production data, supply chain – seamlessly documented.

Real-time Updates

Documents change? The DPP always shows the current version. No more outdated PDFs.

API Integration

Connection to your ERP, WMS or webshop. Automatic data synchronization without manual maintenance.

Audit-Ready

Authority inspection? Customer audit? One scan is enough. Complete documentation at the push of a button.

Timeline

EU Regulation: The DPP Becomes Mandatory

2024

ESPR Adopted

EU Regulation 2024/1781 (Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation) comes into force.

2027

Batteries

First product category with DPP requirement. Industrial batteries and EV batteries must have a digital passport.

2028

Textiles

Clothing and textile products follow. Transparency about materials and production conditions.

2030+

Chemicals & Further Categories

Gradual expansion to other product groups. Those who start early have competitive advantages.
