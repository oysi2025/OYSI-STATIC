Case Studies

Documented Project Results

Anonymized case studies from 2024-2025. Each case shows the initial situation, action taken and measurable result.

Chemical Trading
Mid-sized · 12 suppliers · B2B distribution

From Documentation Chaos to Audit Readiness

Initial Situation

Mid-sized chemical trading company with 12 suppliers, outdated SDS documentation, ADR processes partially unclear. No unified data basis for regulatory requirements.

Action

Documentation audit, standardization of ADR processes, integration into substance database and creation of DPP prerequisites.

Result
  • Audit-ready in 6 weeks
  • 70% fewer authority inquiries
  • Approval time for new products halved
Laboratory & Production
Production line · CLP · REACH

Regulatory Delays Eliminated

Initial Situation

Production line with regulatory delays in CLP labeling and REACH compliance checks. Certification processes blocked market launches.

Action

Full compliance review, systematic CLP refinement, exposure scenarios optimized and documented.

Result
  • 35% faster certification
  • Error rate in SDS creation reduced to below 1%
Logistics & Dangerous Goods
ADR transport · Supply chain

Authority Complaints Reduced to Zero

Initial Situation

ADR transport documents inconsistent, repeated complaints during authority inspections. Risk of transport stops and fines.

Action

ADR checklist introduced, supply chain audit conducted, transport documents completely restructured.

Result
  • 90% fewer form errors
  • Smooth spot-check-free transports
