About OYSI

From garage to European chemical partner – 30 years of expertise in technical chemistry.

Our Story

In 1995, Olivier Höfer founded his first company in a garage in Saarbrücken. From these humble beginnings, he developed Höfer Chemie GmbH into one of Germany's leading chemical distributors – with 70 employees and over 11,000 m² of production and logistics space.

2025 marks a new chapter: with OYSI GmbH, Olivier Höfer combines all his expertise from 30 years in the chemical industry. The focus is on strategic B2B chemical trading with proprietary digital infrastructure and the highest compliance standards.

"After 30 years in the chemical industry, I know one thing: it's not the product that makes the difference, but knowledge, service, and compliance."
— Olivier Höfer, CEO

Our Values

Expertise: 30 years of industry experience in all areas of technical chemistry – from acids and bases to specialty chemicals.

Compliance: Full EU compliance with REACH, CLP/GHS, and ADR. Every product with complete documentation.

Digital: Proprietary digital infrastructure with the Digital Product Passport (DPP) and a public chemistry lexicon with over 3,800 substances.

Personal: Direct advice from an expert – no call centers, no waiting.
